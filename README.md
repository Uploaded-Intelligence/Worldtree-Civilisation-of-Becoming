# Worldtree Civilisation of Becoming

> A local-first game for growing a Worldtree Civilisation of Becoming from
> lived creations, evidence, and possibility.

**Status: separated foundation only. No playable application or participant
system exists yet.**

Worldtree begins solo. A Player articulates horizons, makes lived Creations and
occurrences tangible, sees their civilisation grow, and encounters possibility
branches that remain hypotheses until consciously ratified. As the ecology
matures, the same process lineage may support consensual resonance and
federation without turning people into profiles or compatibility scores.

## Relationship to Intersectionalities of Becoming

Worldtree is a first-party game expression of the wider Becoming ecology. This
repository owns play, visual persistence, Campaigns, Ascension Paths, and game
feel. The separate `Intersectionalities of Becoming` project owns the
constitutional ontology, consent, authority, provenance, governance, and
federation contracts.

The two projects are intentionally separate repositories with different
development rhythms. Worldtree will pin released semantic contracts rather
than copying the upstream doctrine.

participant data, social discovery, matching, production deployment, or
federation.
