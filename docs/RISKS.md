# Risks and Mitigations

| ID | Risk | Early signal | Mitigation | Stop condition |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| R-01 | Progress becomes productivity pressure | branch count, streaks, or completion language dominates | no universal levels or engagement metrics; include rest, care, refusal, and fallow states | Players report coercive self-optimization as the primary experience |
| R-02 | Game ontology forks upstream meaning | Worldtree invents incompatible consent, authority, or claim semantics | released contract manifest and conformance review | unreconciled semantic contradiction reaches durable data |
| R-03 | Artifact becomes identity proof | polish or frequency influences worth or authenticity | occurrence/interpretation split; equal eligibility invariants | exposure or suggestions systematically reward polish |
| R-04 | Agent hypothesis becomes truth | spectral branches solidify without an explicit Player act | deterministic ratification gate outside the model | any unauthorized durable update |
| R-05 | Private lived material leaks | images or notes appear in logs, analytics, fixtures, or public history | local-first vault, no telemetry, synthetic fixtures, publication scan | confirmed unauthorized disclosure |
| R-06 | The loop feels administratively heavy | form-filling exceeds felt discovery or growth | measure time-to-first-growth and remove nonessential articulation | solo play feels like profile maintenance |
| R-07 | Visual magic outruns semantics | animation implies causal or identity claims not supported by lineage | distinct visual grammar for traces, hypotheses, and ratified branches | Players cannot distinguish what occurred from what was suggested |
| R-08 | Cost router degrades quality | Luna produces generic or unsafe branches | representative evals and deterministic escalation to Terra | material usefulness or safety regression against the baseline |
| R-09 | Cost router silently escalates | routine requests frequently reach Sol/xhigh | explicit policy signals, budgets, and telemetry without content | unexplained tier escalation persists |
| R-10 | Premature federation distorts solo design | social features appear before a useful solo loop | milestone gate and separate approval | networking becomes necessary for value |
| R-11 | Separation publishes unreleased kernel work | upstream schemas or doctrine are copied before Genesis release | no contract snapshot until a tagged upstream release | unpublished upstream material enters public history |
