# Worldtree Civilisation of Becoming — Design Foundation

## Player fantasy

I am a Player playing my Game of Becoming. My lived actions do not disappear
into an activity feed; they grow a visible civilisation that helps me perceive
where I have been, what is becoming possible, and what I may choose to enact
next.

## Spatial thesis

Worldtree is a vast possibility field containing multiple living Campaign
districts. Each Campaign may grow its own Tree—Belonging, Music, Venture, Care,
Exploration, or forms not yet named—while mycorrhizal relations show how growth
in one domain conditions another.

World Horizons are attractors, not final victory states. Ascension Paths may
backpropagate prerequisites from an articulated horizon, but they remain
editable hypotheses rather than compulsory tech trees.

## Growth grammar

- **Empiric / Creation:** a photo, song, dinner, journey, repair, care act,
  conversation, experiment, rest practice, or other tangible trace.
- **Trace bud:** immediate acknowledgement that an occurrence was captured.
- **Spectral branch:** a possibility proposed from evidence but not yet made
  durable.
- **Ratified branch:** a Player-endorsed interpretation, commitment, or enacted
  growth.
- **Campaign district:** a locally coherent field of Becoming, not a fixed
  identity category.
- **Mycorrhizal entanglement:** a relation through which Campaigns condition or
  nourish one another without collapsing into one hierarchy.

## Core solo loop

```text
articulate a horizon
        ↓
live something and capture its trace
        ↓
watch a bud grow immediately
        ↓
encounter several grounded possibilities
        ↓
expand, revise, ratify, refuse, or leave fallow
        ↓
take a small action in the world
        ↺
```

The infrastructure initiates possibilities because human cognition is often
better at expanding a seed than generating one from emptiness. Suggestions
must cite what prompted them, disclose uncertainty, and preserve refusal.

## What makes it a game

- direct, animated, spatially persistent consequences;
- discovery and possibility rather than task administration;
- multiple viable paths without a universal optimum;
- meaningful choices that change the visible world;
- returning with evidence from embodied life, not grinding platform actions;
- growth that can include rest, pruning, composting, repair, and dissolution.

## What the game refuses

- gamified productivity or self-surveillance;
- XP as a proxy for worth;
- streak anxiety and punishment for absence;
- polished artifacts as proof of authentic Becoming;
- algorithmic declarations of identity or destiny;
- engagement loops that displace embodied life;
- autonomous agents acting as the Player.

## Selected visual direction

The selected direction is **Worldtree Civilisation**: a hybrid of sacred Tree
continuity and plural living districts, surrounded by a magical field of near
possibility. The original comparative visual study is preserved at
`docs/design/becoming-world-architecture.html` as design lineage, not runtime
code.
