# What Winning Looks Like

Status: ratified project direction; Separation v0.0.1 is the active campaign
Owner: Uploaded-Intelligence
Last updated: 2026-07-14

## Terminal Goal-State

A Player can grow a personally meaningful, visually persistent Worldtree
Civilisation from lived empirics and Creations. The system helps them articulate
non-final horizons, notice real growth, imagine adjacent possibilities, and
choose reversible enactments without reducing Becoming to productivity,
identity labels, levels, scores, or model-authored truth.

The game is useful solo on first use. Network effects later add reciprocal,
consensual resonance without making social scale a prerequisite for value.

## Peak Winning

Worldtree becomes a living game-space where evidence of embodied life grows a
Civilisation of Becoming; multiple Campaign Trees remain distinct yet
mycorrhizally entangled; agents expand possibility without becoming sovereign;
and relationships formed through the ecology remain accountable, portable, and
capable of surviving platform exit.

Winning is demonstrated when the Player gains self-endorsed affordances and
enacts Becomings that were previously difficult to perceive or initiate.
Engagement, branch count, productivity, popularity, or time in the game are not
terminal metrics.

## Current Campaign: Separation v0.0.1

Separation wins when:

- Worldtree exists as its own local Codex workspace and GitHub repository;
- a new agent can determine the project telos, boundaries, current truth, risks,
  next milestone, and verification path without reading prior conversations;
- the game-specific design lineage is preserved without copying chat logs;
- `Intersectionalities of Becoming` remains the explicit semantic kernel;
- the older `Diegetic Game of Becoming` scaffold is superseded but untouched;
- no product runtime or unreleased upstream ontology is accidentally published.

## Non-Negotiable Constraints

- Worldtree is a first-party ecology client, not a fork or replacement ontology.
- Occurrence, interpretation, hypothesis, ratification, enactment, and outcome
  remain distinct.
- The Player controls durable meaning and growth.
- Private artifacts remain local unless separately and explicitly authorized.
- No identity inference, universal victory state, compatibility score,
  authenticity score, engagement objective, or permanent reputation.
- Public history contains synthetic material only.

## Non-Goals

- Implementing the playable seed during Separation v0.0.1.
- Model-backed possibility generation.
- Authentication, backend storage, deployment, matching, or federation.
- Publishing an unreleased snapshot of the upstream ontology.
- Migrating, renaming, committing, or deleting the legacy Diegetic folder.

## Failure Modes

1. Separation becomes a second ontology rather than a game client.
2. Game progression reproduces productivity or self-optimization pressure.
3. Captured artifacts are treated as proof of identity or authenticity.
4. Spectral model suggestions become durable truth without ratification.
5. A visually impressive world obscures an effortful or administratively heavy
   lived loop.
6. Repo scaffolding is mistaken for a playable product.
7. Context is lost when work moves into the new Codex folder.

## Open Decisions

- The exact first-play interaction and visual direction will be tested in the
  Playable Seed campaign, not decided through architecture alone.
- Model-backed possibility generation remains gated on deterministic baseline
  evidence and evaluation.
- Hosting, participant-data architecture, and federation remain later gates.
