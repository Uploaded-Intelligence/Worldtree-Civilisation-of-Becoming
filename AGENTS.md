# Worldtree Lead-Orchestrator Contract

## Project telos

Build a local-first Game of Becoming in which a Player grows a living
Worldtree Civilisation from lived occurrences, Creations, commitments, and
carefully distinguished possibility hypotheses.

Start every substantial task by reading `docs/WINNING.md`. Keep
`docs/PROGRESS.md` as the canonical live state and update it at every dispatch,
verification, review, integration, or blocker transition.

## Ecological boundary

This repository owns the game experience: Worldtree projections, Campaigns,
Ascension Paths, artifact capture, possibility-space, game feel, and solo play.

`Intersectionalities of Becoming` remains the upstream constitutional and
semantic kernel. It owns the ontogenetic grammar, consent, standing, mandate,
authority, provenance, governance, and federation contracts. Worldtree may
consume a released, versioned contract; it must not silently fork that kernel.

## Non-negotiable design laws

1. A game metaphor is a situated projection, not a total model of a person.
2. Captured evidence establishes that something was recorded, not what it means.
3. A possibility branch remains spectral until the Player ratifies it.
4. Similarity, polish, frequency, or model confidence never establishes worth,
   authenticity, identity, compatibility, or synergy.
5. Models may propose interpretations; they may not authorize, ratify, publish,
   contact, commit, delete, or mutate durable lineage.
6. Rest, care, maintenance, refusal, unfinished work, disabled modes, collective
   work, and non-digital activity remain valid forms of Becoming.
7. No streaks, popularity metrics, universal levels, engagement optimization,
   or productivity hierarchy.

## Model routing

- Deterministic code for validation, replay, state transitions, authorization,
  ratification, deletion, and policy.
- GPT-5.6 Luna for bounded high-volume generation after evaluation.
- GPT-5.6 Terra for complex synthesis and primary implementation.
- GPT-5.6 Sol at high or xhigh only for ontology, safety, architecture, and
  final adversarial review.
- A model cannot grant itself a stronger model, more context, or more authority.

## Operating system

- Use `codex/*` branches for non-trivial changes.
- Concurrent implementers require separate git worktrees.
- Use test-first development for behavior.
- Require fresh verification and spec/code-quality review before completion.
- Keep public history synthetic and free of participant data, secrets, personal
  artifacts, and conversation transcripts.
- Stop for destructive actions, credentials, deployment, participant-data use,
  consequential model authority, external publication beyond the agreed repo,
  finance, or unclear product intent.
